PhD Student (Graduated)

Mansoor Soomro (16Ph.DEnSE 05)

Research Topic: Development of controller design for power quality improvement of grid integrated wind energy conversion system.

The research focused on improving power quality and fault ride-through capability of grid integrated wind energy conversion systems (WECS). Advanced controller strategies were developed for DFIG based systems under symmetrical and asymmetrical fault conditions.

Funding: Mr. Mansoor Soomro received a 2.0 Million PKR Endowment Scholarship Grant for pursuing PhD studies at Mehran University of Engineering and Technology (MUET), Jamshoro, Pakistan.

PhD Student (Graduated)

Ms. Kaneez Fatima (18IICTPhD28)

Research Topic: Computational Investigations for Clean and Renewable Hydrogen Energy, its Storage and Applications Based on First-Principles Calculations.

This research investigated novel hydrogen storage materials based on 2D mono atomic hexagonal boron nitrides (h-BN) and transition metal nitrides (TMN) using Density Functional Theory (DFT) and ab-initio Molecular Dynamics (MD) simulations. The work demonstrated that Li-doped h-BN materials provide thermally stable and efficient hydrogen adsorption and desorption properties suitable for hydrogen energy applications.

Funding: This research was supported through an approved project funded by the Pakistan Science Foundation (PSF) titled:

“Computational investigations on Novel 2D Mono atomic hexagonal boron nitrides (h-BN) and transition metal nitrides (TMN) materials for hydrogen storage application.”

Letter No.: PSF/Res/S-MUET/Engg/(187), May 2021.
